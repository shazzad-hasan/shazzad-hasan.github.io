Shazzad Hasan

Hi! I'm Shazzad Hasan. I'm currently pursuing an MSc in Scientific Computing and Data Analysis with a specialization in Financial Technology at Durham University, UK.

I love learning from first principles, writing efficient code, training machine learning and deep learning algorithms on large datasets, and exploring causal effects and science that connects the dots.

Right now, I'm deepening my knowledge of Financial Mathematics, Blockchain Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Data Analysis and Software Development. My ultimate goal is to build a career in the Financial Engineering domain.

I completed my BS in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from North South University, Bangladesh with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence. Initially I was interested in control systems engineering. During my undergraduate studies, I had the privilege to take control engineering courses with Dr. Lamia Iftekhar and work as a Research Assistant with Dr. Mohammad Monir Uddin. I spent one year with Prof. Monir working on model order reduction of large scale structured dynamical system by projecting the system onto the dominant eigenspace of the Gramian(PDEG) and solved Lyapunov Matrix equation by Rational Krylov subspace method (RKSM) and Alternating-direction implicit method (ADI).
